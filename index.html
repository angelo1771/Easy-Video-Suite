A lot of marketers find it difficult to convert their hard earned website traffic into sales, and it can be a really frustrating situation to be in ... when you see visitors coming to your website (often after paying a lot of time and money to get them there), only to watch them click away and never sign up to your lists or buy from you.

If you ever want to make big money from the Internet, you need to find a way to convert your visitors into subscribers and buyers ... and fast!

One of the biggest issues, which is causing a lack of sales, is the lack of trust your visitors have in you. They've got no reason to believe what you say, they don't know who you are, and ultimately people who feel like this towards you are never going to give you money.

Marketers realise this, and the web is changing fast to solve this problem. You may have noticed that as more more people use modern technology on their websites, your old textbased websites are starting to look dated and dull. More to the point, it's very hard to trust a website that has only got text on it.

In the old days, sales were made by people knocking on doors and speaking face-to-face with people...

And that psychology has never died, in fact ... it's becoming more and more essential on the Internet.

But how do you speak face-to-face with somebody when you live in a different country, and they are visiting your website 3 AM and you're in bed?

The answer, is video.

The web is becoming more transparent, and people want to know who they're buying from. Just like in the olden days, where salesmen would visit people face-to-face ... video allows a very close replication of that sales situation.

the buyer can see your face (or at least hear your voice), they can trust you are a real person, they can share your sarcasm and humour, they can get a clear idea of who you are and how genuine you are... something that text alone cannot do.

I'm not saying that text only based websites don't work any more, but video is quickly taking over the web and those that get left behind will struggle to compete when it comes to making sales and getting subscribers.

With video, it allows you to build trust with your visitors (and build a relationship where they end up buying from you).

It allows you to express yourself in ways that text alone cannot (which can increase sales and keep people's attention)

Plus, the viral nature of videos allows people to share your content, and get you more traffic as a result. There's just so many things you can do with video that you cannot do with text only websites.

But the biggest thing of all, is that it's easy to keep people's attention, teach them things, connect on a personal level... all without having to take up pages and pages on your website.

It really is the most efficient, proven way to get a response from your website visitors.
